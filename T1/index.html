Jio fastest mobile network with 19.3 Mbps average download speed: TRAI

Reliance Jio continues to remain the fastest mobile network with an average download speed of 19.3 Mbps in September, according toTRAI data. Idea Cellular network (now Vodafone Idea) followed Jio with a download speed of 8.6Mbps, Vodafone 7.9 Mbps and Bharti Airtel 7.5 Mbps. Though Vodafone and Idea have merged their mobile buisnesses, TRAI measured their performance separately.
